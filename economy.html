New start up selling elephant milk

"Elephant Essence," founded by an ambitious group of entrepreneurs, promises to deliver a unique dairy alternative that is both creamy and rich in nutrients. The company claims that elephant milk has a higher fat content than cow's milk, along with a variety of vitamins and minerals. “We’ve always loved elephants, and now we’re finding a way to share their unique gift with the world,” said founder Zara Qureshi. The startup has partnered with sanctuaries to ethically source milk, ensuring the elephants are treated with the utmost care and respect. Despite the novelty, critics have raised concerns about the sustainability of milking elephants and the potential ethical dilemmas. However, fans of the product are already lining up for a taste of this exotic dairy.
